Alfredo Pasta

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make alfredo pasta and you will leave this learning how to make one

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Cheese - freshly grated
  • Butter - Salted
  • Pasta
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

How to make it

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the packaging
  2. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the garlic and cook for 30 second, or until fragrant
  3. Pour int the milk and cream.Stir consistently to avoid burning on the bottom of the pan until the mixture comes to a boil. Turn the heat down to medium, and mix in the parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.Adjeust the seasoning your own taste
  4. Remove the pan from the heat and mix in the cooked pasta until the sauce begins to thicken.Garnish with parsley, and serve
