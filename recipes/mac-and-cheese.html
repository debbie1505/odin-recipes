Mac and Cheese

Description

This recipe will teach you how to mac and cheese so that you flaunt your cooking skills at the next thanksgiving dinner

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter and flourr
  • Dry mustard. nutmeg, black pepper, and cayenne
  • onion
  • Milk and heavy cream
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Cheeses

How to make it

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 fahrenheit
  2. Boil the pasta
  3. Make the bechamel sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Bake macaroni and cheese for 30 minutes
